# HR-Analytics-Dashboard

## Objective

Analyze employee distribution and employee status using Google Sheets.

## Tools Used

- Google Sheets

## Google Sheets Dashboard

- Google Sheets Link:https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-yOX4xl3VQsA-5S1PW73_yYdVY0WEKhnh51CRBc_NZU/edit?usp=sharing

## Key Findings

- Total Employees: 2845
- Active Employees: 86.4%
- Terminated Employees: 13.6%
- Production Department has the highest number of employees
- Employees are distributed almost equally across business units

## Conclusion

The organization maintains a stable workforce with a high percentage of active employees and balanced workforce distribution across business units.
